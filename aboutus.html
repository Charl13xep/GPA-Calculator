About Us

Our Mission

At GPA Genius, our mission is to simplify the process of calculating and tracking academic performance for students aspiring to achieve their educational goals.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every student has the tools and resources needed to accurately assess and improve their academic standing, unlocking their full potential.

Our Story

Our journey began when we recognized the challenges students face in managing their academic achievements. With diverse backgrounds in education and technology, we united to develop a solution that not only simplifies GPA calculation but also helps students visualize their academic progress and plan for the future.
